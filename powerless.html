Ashlesha’s Book Read list

Powerless!

I love this series so much! If I had to rank it then i would give it a 9.5/10 just becuase it made me cry. The way Lauren writes is geniunely so romantic that it makes my heart ache everytime I read this series. My favorite characters in the whole series probably had to be Jax becuase he literally had no idea what was going on and we love a unbothered king!

Book 1

So Book one is Powerless and let me just say that at first the book was boring. Like I didn't understand the start until I read the end which kinda of pissed me off but its ok becuase it was definately worth it. I would reccommend this book to younger people, probably 6-7th graders just becuase it can get a little dark at times. Out of all the books in this series this is going probably second.

Book 2

Book 2 honestly made me cry so much, like I wasn't even expecting these plot twists like it's actually so crazy to even think about. This definetly goes 1st on my list, wait no, the third book is first. Reckless toward the end started pissing me off a little but its okay because the third book makes up for it completely.

Book 3

Ok so book 3 is probably my favorite one. Like I know this book was kind of boring for some people but honestly KITT IS SUCH A CUTIE PIE. Like he did not deserve to die, thats kinda mean even for him. Like all he wanted was to be happy with his brother but his brother wanted to be happy with Paedyn, its giving TSITP. But honestly 10000000/10 like genuinely chefs freaking kiss. WOULD DEFINATELY RECCOMEND TO READ

There are novellas but explaining them would be really difficult since you have to read the main books in order to understand the novellas.
